Notes:

Configurable Script.
It is working with every mod.
You can skin animals or men or anything else modded.
You can select the dropped items ( meat or anything ) to be from ,
Ravage or Heros Survive mod , or any other mod ,
by adding the classnames of the dropped items.

Once you are close to the victim , you will get an addaction:
"Skin and quarter the body".
You can add your custom sounds when skinning ,
3 sounds are included.
