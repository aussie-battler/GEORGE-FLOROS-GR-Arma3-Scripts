Notes:

Create your desired , Ambient Environment.
You can add your custom sounds.
It is also included a mod version , posible to unpack and edit. 
The sound files including  , day and night ambience according to time.
The script will prevent running , when the player is inside a vehicle or water.
There are sound files for birds , insects and some forest SFX , also 
a day and a night ambient loop.
