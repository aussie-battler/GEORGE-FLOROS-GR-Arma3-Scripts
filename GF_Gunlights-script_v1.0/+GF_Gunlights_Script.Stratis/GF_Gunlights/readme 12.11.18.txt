Notes:

GF Gunlights Script , configurable script for AI.
The usage of the flashlight will vary , according the behaviour of the  current AI ,
as ex : "AWARE" , the time of the day and the indoor usage
You can add in the init of a unit to exclude :
this setVariable ["Var_GF_Gunlights", true];
