GF Custom Loot Position Spawn Script


Description:

GF Custom Loot Position Spawn Script , spawn Weapons , Mags , Items , to the desired position , configurable script.
You are free to do anything but i would like to give me Credits for this!
Simple and easy to use and adapt .
Have Fun !


Create a Custom Loot Position Spawn

Open this mission to the 3D editor and copy paste ( ctrl c - ctrl v ) the item "Land_BakedBeans_F" ,
wherever you want , the loot to be spawned .


You can also paste this code in any item , but i think , the best selection , is the Land_BakedBeans_F , 
because the selected item , that you will use , will interact with the height of the spawned items.


Below , i have create a vanilla ( from number 1. - 9. ) and ravage Loot List ( from number 10. - 13. )
and number 0 is for spawning objects.
In the editor , i have place , the item , baked beans , from left to right,
In front of this table , you will see the baked beans , that spawn these items.
Left from the table , you will see the baked beans , that spawn objects .


These are the codes in the init field of the item  baked beans.


*** IMPORTANT NOTE *** : 
In order to get everything spawned correctly you must follow , the arrays , 
for what sort of list items are spawned , from this certain item ( beans ).
In the init field of every code , it has been used a certain word

addweaponcargo
addItemCargo
addMagazineCargoGlobal
addMagazineCargo
addBackpackCargoGlobal


It's wrong to try to spawn ex: Attachments  ( addItemCargo ) , 
to another array ( init field of the item ).

You can merge and change the lists , but check what exacly ,
this array spawns.


In the Future updates there will be also added 
Loot List also , for other addons.

Objects_array =selectRandom[

			"Box_IND_WpsSpecial_F"

Spawn_Objects = createVehicle [Objects_array, this, [], 0, "NONE"];
Spawn_Objects setDir random 360;
Spawn_Objects attachTo [this, [0,0,0.2]]; 
detach Spawn_Objects;   
deleteVehicle this;
